Project Description

View Details
Introducing eContabil! A short overview of the product and how it came about.

Identifying The Problem

Why would anyone ever want to be bothered with the business financial activities? This is something that accountants or a finance department should take care of, and it should just happen, right? We all know that invoicing, bookkeeping, tax reporting and banking require a lot of time and dealing with banks, accountants and wating in lines is not the most fun thing in the world, especially in Romania, but we want to change that forever.

Our Solution

eContabil offers freelancers, small business owners a place where they can feel safe and rest assured that their finances, tax reporting and banking are dealt with.

Customer segment

1. The Too-Busy-To Budget Bunch

Creating a budget and sticking to it is easier said than done. The modern working professional has too much to do, too much going on, and too much to worry about to religiously keep track of receipts and purchases. These busy bees are most at risk of over-spending or inadvertently wasting money simply because they’re not tracking what they’re buying. That’s why they’re relying on our app to do the budgeting for them.


2. The Forgetful Few

From cable bills to heating bills, upcoming rent and loan repayment deadlines, many have a fair amount of bills they regularly pay. To make matters more complicated, these bills usually have separate due dates and penalty structures. It’s impossible to keep track of everything you owe and when. Plus, it can be incredibly frustrating when you accidentally miss a payment. Our app keeps tabs on bills, and we likely have a segment of users that always cut it close when a deadline approaches. So we make bill tracking easier for people by setting up app reminders.


3. The Do-it-Yourself Taxpayers

Let’s be honest: doing your taxes can be a big, form-filling, number-crunching headache. Some people work with a tax professional to file their return, while others brave the process on their own with a trusty app. Our mobile mission is to help people survive tax season, build a segment of users who have self-selected to independently file their taxes in our app.

Planning out Customer discovery

As our method of customer discovery, as an initial stage of project validation we used a Google form to find out what type of clients fits our project. Since our project is based in Romania, we chose to create the form in Romanian so our potential clients are more accustomed to their mother language. As a starting point in our form we wanted to find out an age segment and education status of our potential customers.

The second part was about identifying how they interacted with tax payments in the past, what they used and how satisfied they were with what they used.

The third part of our form was focused on previous experiences that impacted their lives, whether they were positive or negative, and their level of satisfaction regarding the subject.

As a result of this form we found out that our ideal age group is 18-25 with the least represented groups being below 18, which is normal since they don’t usually have to make any payments, and 40+. Most of the answers regarding how they made any payments before was that they already have an accountant that takes care of taxes, and least amount of people used any online solutions.
